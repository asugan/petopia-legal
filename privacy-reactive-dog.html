Privacy Policy - Petopia Reactive Dog Assistant

Last Updated: February 11, 2026
Effective Date: February 11, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Petopia Reactive Dog, the specialized companion for owners of reactive and sensitive dogs. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your information when you use our mobile application ("Petopia Reactive Dog" or "App").

Our app is specifically designed to help owners track reactivity triggers, log training progress, manage threshold levels, and create a safer environment for their pets.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

Account Information:

  • Email address and password (for authentication)
  • Pet profiles including breed, age, and specific reactivity types
  • Training logs and progress notes
  • Custom trigger lists (e.g., specific noises, other animals, vehicles)
  • Threshold observations and stress level logs
  • Photos and videos of training sessions or pet behavior

Behavioral Data:

  • Logs of reactive incidents and their context
  • Counter-conditioning and desensitization progress
  • Success rates of specific management strategies
  • Medication and supplement schedules related to behavior

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

Usage & Device Data:

  • App interaction patterns (features used most often)
  • Device type, OS version, and crash reports
  • Approximate location (via IP) to provide localized training tips

Storage Data:

  • Local database of training logs
  • Cached media for quick access to training videos

2.3 Third-Party Services

Payments & Subscriptions:

  • RevenueCat: Manages premium training features and subscriptions.

Infrastructure:

  • Better Auth: Secure login and session management.
  • Expo Updates: Technical performance monitoring.

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Core Functionality

  • Trigger Tracking: Identifying patterns in your dog's reactivity.
  • Progress Analysis: Visualizing improvements in threshold management over time.
  • Customized Plans: Suggesting management strategies based on logged triggers.
  • Reminders: Notifications for training sessions and behavior medication.

3.2 Safety and Support

  • Providing data-driven insights to help you discuss behavior with professionals.
  • Improving app stability through diagnostic logs.

4. Data Storage and Security

Reactivity data is sensitive. We prioritize local storage where possible. Syncing to the cloud is encrypted and used only to ensure you don't lose your hard-earned training progress when switching devices.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your dog's behavioral data or your personal information.

We only share data with essential service providers (like authentication or cloud backup) under strict confidentiality agreements.

6. Your Rights

You have the full right to access, export, or permanently delete your account and all associated pet data directly from the app settings.

Apple App Store Data Safety Summary

  • Data linked to you: Email address, pet profiles, behavior logs.
  • Data not linked: Diagnostic data and performance metrics.
  • No Tracking: We do not track you across other apps for advertising.
