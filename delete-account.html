Petopia – Account Deletion Request

This page explains how users can request the deletion of their Petopia account and associated personal data.

How to delete your account

  • Send an email to petopia@dekadans.net
  • Use the same email address that is linked to your Petopia account
  • Include the subject line: Account Deletion Request

Your request will be reviewed and processed within 7 days.

Data that will be deleted

  • Account information (email address, user ID)
  • Pet profiles and reminders
  • App usage data linked to your account

Data retention

Certain data, such as purchase and subscription records, may be retained for up to 90 days to comply with legal and accounting requirements.

Contact

If you have questions about data deletion, please contact us at petopia@dekadans.net.
